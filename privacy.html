Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026  |  Last updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kevin Aust ("I", "me", or "my"), operating as Kevin The Magician, collects, uses, and handles information in connection with the video publishing application used to post content to TikTok and other social media platforms.

1. What This App Does

This is a personal automation tool that uploads pre-recorded magic performance videos to TikTok and other social media platforms on a scheduled basis. It is not a public-facing product or service.

2. Information Collected

To publish content to TikTok, this application accesses the following through the TikTok API:

  • Account access tokens – OAuth tokens granted by TikTok to authorize video uploads on behalf of the account.
  • Video content – Pre-recorded video files prepared for upload.
  • Basic profile information – As returned by the TikTok API (e.g., username, user ID) solely to confirm the correct account is being used.

No personal data from viewers or followers is collected, stored, or processed.

3. How Information Is Used

All data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with the TikTok API using OAuth 2.0.
  • Upload video files to the designated TikTok account.
  • Schedule posts at predetermined times.

No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising or analytics.

4. Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens are stored locally on a personal device and are not transmitted to any external server other than TikTok's own API endpoints. Video files are temporarily hosted for upload and are not retained after successful posting.

5. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as needed to maintain the publishing workflow. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account security settings at tiktok.com/settings/security.

6. Third-Party Services

This application interacts with TikTok's API, which is subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

Because this is a personal tool and no end-user data is collected, there is no personal data to request, correct, or delete beyond revoking the OAuth authorization through TikTok directly.
